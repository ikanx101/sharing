Biography

Mohammad Rizka Fadhli, M.Si. is a seasoned data scientist, market researcher, and applied mathematician with over 18 years of professional experience across FMCG, telecommunications, banking, and e-commerce. He currently serves as Market Research Specialist at PT Nutrifood Indonesia, where he independently develops and maintains end-to-end commercial analytics solutions — spanning marketing ROI analysis, demand forecasting, customer retention modeling, and raw-material procurement optimization — using mathematical approaches including Monte Carlo Simulation, Mixed Marketing Models, and integer programming. In parallel, he provides direct mentoring and capacity-building to Nutrifood employees across various departments, equipping them with analytical skills to independently function as data analysts within their respective business units. His pioneering contributions to building a data-driven culture at Nutrifood were recognized with the CEO Award 2025. He is a regular guest lecturer at ITB and Telkom University, a certified Big Data Analyst (TUV-Rheinland), and an active member of AIDI (Asosiasi Ilmuwan Data Indonesia).
